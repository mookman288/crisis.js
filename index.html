crisis.js

A library for websites that encounter individuals experiencing crisis

Examples

English
  • Suicide
    • hurt myself

  • Abuse/Violence
    • hitting me

    • I'm being stalked
  • Sexual Assault/Rape
    • she raped me
    • he raped me
    • they've been groping me

Resources

Currently the system is designed to forward a user to one of these resources by default:

  • Suicide: http://www.suicide.org/if-you-are-suicidal.html
  • Abuse/Violence: http://www.thehotline.org/help/
  • Sexual Assault/Rape: https://rainn.org/get-help
